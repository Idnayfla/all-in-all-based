## Long Description (for PH listing body)

**Based is the AI companion that never leaves your side.**

Most AI tools do one thing. You have ChatGPT for questions, Midjourney for images, a separate video editor, a separate code sandbox, a separate note-taking app.

Based does all of it. In one place. With one AI that remembers who you are.

**What Based does:**

- **Builds apps** — describe what you want, Based generates HTML/CSS/JS with a live preview. Games, dashboards, tools, calculators. Runs in the browser, no setup.
- **Generates images** — FLUX and Nano Banana, text-to-image and image editing. Describe it, get it.
- **Edits video** — trim, text overlays, speed control, AI command bar. Type plain English, Based edits.
- **Composes music** — describe a track, pick a genre, get a real audio file. Cinematic, lo-fi, jazz, epic — whatever you need.
- **Answers anything** — Claude-powered chat with web search, memory, and full context.
- **Remembers everything** — AI memory across every session. Knows your projects, your style, your preferences.
- **Notes** — rich text, drawing canvas, cross-device sync.
- **Windows companion** — floating overlay that lives on your desktop.

**Why Based exists:**

AI tools are fragmented. You pay for five subscriptions, switch between them constantly, and still spend half your time managing the tools instead of making things.

Based was built from Singapore to collapse that stack into one companion you actually want to use.

**Pricing:**

- Free — 10 generations/month, 3 projects, chat + code
- Pro — $12/month. Unlimited builds, all media generation, AI memory, voice activation, priority speed

**Built by one person. Funded by the community.**

Every Pro subscription directly funds the next feature. The roadmap is public. The bugs you report get fixed. That's the deal.
